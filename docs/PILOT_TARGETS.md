## The 10 slots (approach in this order)
| # | Archetype | Why this slot | Free-layer hook that lands first |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1–2 | **Multi-activity kids hub / enrichment center** (art+music+movement+open play under one roof) | Bullseye ICP — the segment no incumbent fits (R-002); worst current tooling | Booking page: one link for ALL their programs |

| 8 | **Kids art studio** | Small, spreadsheet-run, fast yes/no; photos = the product itself | Photo moments + requests inbox |
| 9 | **Church preschool / Mother's Day Out program** | Low budget but massive word-of-mouth density among target parents | Free forever layer; announcements |
| 10 | **Drop-in hourly childcare / play space** | Check-in/out compliance need; closest to brightwheel turf — learn the boundary | Kiosk + daily notes |

## Qualification checklist (all must hold before a slot is filled)
- Toddler/preschool (0–5) programs are core, not incidental · 30–300 enrolled kids · 1–3 locations
- Owner-operated and reachable (active Instagram or answers the phone)
- Current tooling = group texts / IG DMs / paper / spreadsheets / visibly dated portal
- Within driving distance for in-person concierge onboarding (the AgapayCare motion)

## Sourcing playbook (fills the names in ~30 min per archetype)
1. Google Maps: "kids enrichment center / play café / swim school / [archetype] near <metro>" — shortlist 3 per slot by review count 20–300 (big enough to matter, small enough to answer).
2. Instagram: check last-30-day activity; group-text/DM-based signups visible in bio/stories = qualified.
3. Local parent Facebook groups: search "[archetype] recommendations" — recurring names = demand-side proof.
4. Founder network: any AgapayCare-adjacent owner contacts or parent friends who attend one → move that name to slot 1 regardless of archetype (warm beats perfect).

## Outreach script (concierge pitch, aligned to P.8/P.9)
"I run a small software company. We built a free page for [business] where parents book classes
and join waitlists, plus photo updates parents love — no app for them to install, nothing for
your staff to learn (if it needs a manual, we failed). I'll set the whole thing up for you in 30
minutes, free, this week. If it doesn't make your life easier, delete it and keep the page."
— No mention of the paid tier until they're active (P.8: upsell moments come from usage).
