## The gap (load-bearing conclusion)
"Toddler activity hub" = one venue running MULTIPLE program types (art + swim + karate + open-play
+ camps). The market splits into class-roster tools (iClassPro/Jackrabbit — single-vertical
workflow DNA), POS/ticketing tools (ROLLER — walk-in DNA), and licensed-childcare tools
(brightwheel — compliance DNA). A multi-activity hub needs a slice of each and fits none; owners
run 2–3 tools or spreadsheets. No incumbent leads with (a) toddler-specific multi-activity
operations, (b) concierge/managed onboarding, or (c) a genuinely modern mobile-first staff PWA.
Price floor to undercut: ~$79–139/mo entry tiers; migration fees ($300–800) are a switching wound
we exploit with free concierge migration.

## alh-tracker (AgapayCare) pattern study — the business flow to mirror
Three surfaces: (1) internal CRM /crm — desktop-only, business staff: pipeline (subscription
status, onboarding stages), facility list/detail, follow-ups w/ priority+escalation, comm log,
archive; provisioning → app handoff. (2) tenant app — mobile-first, self-serve signup on /home,
operational logging (roster, observations, follow-ups, shift close). (3) family portal — Phase 2,
view-only, counsel-gated, visibility fields already in schema. Stack: React18+TS+Vite+Tailwind+
Zustand+Router on Vercel + Supabase (RLS, migrations); Playwright e2e incl. authenticated
live-journey runner; no app stores. Amityx mapping: facility→hub, resident→child, family→parents.
