# B-002: /crm internal tool — three minor a11y polish misses (contrast, unlabeled filter, small targets)
Severity: S3 minor
Found by: TR-009 while testing T-009 (P.9 usability sweep, axe-core)
Status: open

## Repro steps
1. Sign in as an active `crm_admin` and open `/crm` then `/crm/hubs` (375px).
2. Run axe-core (WCAG 2 a/aa) + a 44px touch-target sweep.

## Expected vs actual
Three WCAG AA / P.9-rule-9 misses, ALL on the internal, staff-only, desktop-first
`/crm` surface (none on any customer-facing screen):
(a) **Contrast 4.34:1** — `text-primary` links (#B54A24) sit on the `muted` surface
    (#F1E9DE): "See all hubs"/"Manage hubs" on `/crm` (×2) and hub-name links on
    `/crm/hubs` (×11). AA text needs 4.5:1. DESIGN.md §3 verified primary-on-background
    (4.91) and primary-on-card (5.29) but NOT primary-on-muted — a token-pairing gap.
Expected: ≥4.5:1. Actual: 4.34:1.
(b) **Unlabeled select** — the subscription-status filter `<select>` on `/crm/hubs`
    has no `<label>`/`aria-label` (axe `select-name`, light + dark). A screen-reader
    user hears only "combobox".
(c) **Sub-44px targets** — the row "Open {hub}" chevron links (16×16) and the two
    dashboard quick-links (~20px tall) are below the 44px target floor. (Desktop mouse
    tool; still a P.9-rule-9 note.)

## Evidence
- axe (crm-home @375): `color-contrast(2)` — "insufficient color contrast of 4.34
  (foreground #b74b24, background #f1eadf, 14px normal). Expected 4.5:1".
- axe (crm-hubs @375): `color-contrast(11)` + `select-name(1)` "Element does not have
  an implicit (wrapped) <label>"; also present at 375 dark (`select-name(1)`).
- touch sweep: crm-home 2 links (99×20, 109×20); crm-hubs 12 links (16×16 chevrons).
- Screens: `app/web/e2e/screens/crm-home-375*.png`, `crm-hubs-375*.png`.
Customer-facing screens (landing, /signup, /h/{slug}, /g/{token}, /app Today/Roster/
Kiosk) had ZERO serious/critical axe violations and no icon-only buttons.

## Fix design (planner — deferred, not cycle-blocking)
Not fixed by the tester: (a) is a design-token decision (either add a `primary-strong`
token for text-on-muted, darken `primary` ~one step and re-verify DESIGN.md §3's whole
contrast table, or stop placing `text-primary` on `muted` in CrmHome/CrmHubs) — a
design-system change that should route through the design skill, not a rushed patch.
(b)+(c) are trivial (add `aria-label`/`<Label className="sr-only">` to the filter
select; give the chevron links `min-h-11 min-w-11` padding). All three are S3/S4 on the
internal staff tool and do NOT block the cycle (pass requires zero S1/S2). Batch into a
small `/crm` a11y polish task; redeploy after.
